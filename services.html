Services

USL provides a number of services around the development and delivery of software.

Software Development

USL has a range of expertise and experience to take on software development projects. These include bespoke document and workflow management systems, content-managed offline-capable mobile apps, and fully-featured Learning Content Management Systems (LCMS).

Hosting and Technical Services

USL delivers the full range of services available in the Microsoft Azure Cloud Hosting platform, ensuring scalability and cost-efficiency. We also provide services for domain names and URL routing.

Consultancy

USL can facilitate Requirements Capture and User Definition Workshops for your team. Whether for new product features, changes in workflow, or new systems, we deliver an outline specification and an Iteration Plan.

Audit

USL offers a Software and Technology Audit service to assess vulnerabilities. This service is particularly beneficial for investors, venture capitalists, and businesses looking for due diligence in their software systems.
